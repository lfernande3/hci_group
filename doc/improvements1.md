# Observation Rubric - Findings and Recommendations

## 1. Visibility of System Status

### Findings from Students
- Laundry colors (green/blue) clearly showed machine states
- Countdown timers were easy to follow
- Booking confirmation disappeared too quickly

### Insights & Recommendations
Maintain persistent feedback and add a "View My Bookings" shortcut.

---

## 2. Match Between System and Real World

### Findings from Students
- "Stacks with one free" confused some users
- Room labels and event tags felt realistic
- Icons for washer/dryer were intuitive

### Insights & Recommendations
Use simpler terms like "Available washerâ€“dryer pair" and keep consistent labels.

---

## 3. User Control and Freedom

### Findings from Students
- Customizable navbar was liked but lacked undo
- Printing couldn't go back after upload
- Wanted option to cancel laundry alerts

### Insights & Recommendations
Add undo and restore functions; allow returning to previous steps.

---

## 4. Consistency and Standards

### Findings from Students
- Dark theme and icons consistent
- Time formats varied between Events and Booking
- Slight differences in button size and padding

### Insights & Recommendations
Standardize timestamps and button dimensions.

---

## 5. Error Prevention

### Findings from Students
- Tried NFC verify before enabling it
- Laundry alerts triggered twice when tapped fast
- No warning for offline Print Submission

### Insights & Recommendations
Add NFC pre-check and prevent duplicate taps.

---

## 6. Recognition Rather Than Recall

### Findings from Students
- Laundry and Events had clear actions
- Print Submit button not prominent
- Booking tabs were self-explanatory

### Insights & Recommendations
Emphasize main buttons and add step indicators.

---

## 7. Flexibility and Efficiency of Use

### Findings from Students
- Suggested favorites for rooms/printers
- Laundry filters worked smoothly
- Navbar customization improved efficiency

### Insights & Recommendations
Add "Favorites" or "Recently Used" feature.

---

## 8. Aesthetic and Minimalist Design

### Findings from Students
- Dark UI looked clean and modern
- Small text (Laundry, A/C) low contrast
- Booking grid easy to read

### Insights & Recommendations
Increase contrast and font size for key info.

---

## 9. Help Users Recognize, Diagnose, and Recover from Errors

### Findings from Students
- Unsure how to retry failed Print Upload
- Booking/Laundry had clear retry states
- Visitor NFC lacked failure guidance

### Insights & Recommendations
Add contextual error dialogs and retry prompts.

---

## 10. Help and Documentation

### Findings from Students
- Wanted quick Help icon in new modules
- Onboarding was clear but not replayable
- Tooltips could assist first-time users

### Insights & Recommendations
Add FAQ section and replay tutorial option.
